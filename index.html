How to play

To play the game of cricket you will need a bat and a ball. It is

played on a field with teams of 11 players each. The object of the

game is to score the most runs when your team is batting and when

your team is not batting you are supposed to get the other batters

on the other team out. The rules are from the form of cricket called

test cricket, the traditional form of cricket, however there are more

formats but the rules differ slightly. There are four different

positions in a cricket team, the batsman, bowler, fielder, and the

wicket keeper. I will tell you about them later on in the next few
